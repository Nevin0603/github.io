About Me

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at Peking University, working in arithmetic algebraic geometry, with a particular focus on L-functions, Shimura varieties, and Arakelov geometry.

Research Interests

  • L-functions and Shimura varieties, with an emphasis on integral models of Shimura varieties, generating series on Shimura varieties, and their arithmetic generalizations.
  • Arakelov geometry, particularly in the study of arithmetic intersection numbers of special cycles and line bundles.

Experience

  • 2018–2022: Bachelor in Mathematics, Peking University
  • 2022–Present: PhD in Mathematics, Peking University (ongoing), Advisor: Prof. Xinyi Yuan

Research Papers
